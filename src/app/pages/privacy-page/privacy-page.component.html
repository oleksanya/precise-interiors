Privacy Policy

At Precise Interiors LLC Ltd, we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or engage with our services. We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the UK Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following data about you:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, address, email, phone number, and company name (if applicable).
  • Technical Data: Information such as your IP address, browser type, and browsing actions on our website.
  • Project Information: Details about your construction projects, such as site addresses, project requirements, and timelines.
  • Communication Data: Information from your correspondence with us via email, phone, or other methods.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Provide and manage our construction services.
  • Communicate with you regarding project updates, quotes, and invoices.
  • Improve our website, services, and user experience.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

We may use your personal information for marketing communications, but only if you have provided explicit consent.

3. Legal Basis for Processing Data

The legal grounds for processing your personal data are:

  • Performance of a Contract: To fulfill our agreement with you.
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with any legal requirements (e.g., tax laws).
  • Consent: Where we process data based on your explicit consent (e.g., for marketing purposes).
  • Legitimate Interests: For processing necessary to improve our services and communicate with you.

4. Sharing Your Data

We will not share your personal data with third parties except:

  • If necessary to fulfill our contract (e.g., subcontractors, suppliers).
  • If we are legally required to do so.
  • If you have given explicit consent.
  • To trusted service providers, such as EmailJS, who assist with providing our services. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and can only use your data for the services they perform for us.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it, such as secure servers and encryption. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, so while we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Your Data Rights

Under GDPR and the UK Data Protection Act 2018, you have the following rights:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can ask us to correct any inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: You can ask us to delete your data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Restriction: You can request that we limit the processing of your data in certain cases.
  • Data Portability: You can request to transfer your data to another service provider.
  • Objection: You can object to processing for marketing purposes.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You can withdraw your consent for marketing communications at any time.
